Cookies, Cookies, and More Cookies. There was a time where I was obsessed with COOKIES at a very young age until I learned about the cookie monster. A blue puppet like creature with lifeless eyes staring into your deepest secrets. (Click,if you dare) Cookie Monster Children will immediately recognize this puppet from the show Sesame Street and from our childhood we learn that Cookie Monster loves COOKIES . He is often the beloved puppet children love for his funny looks and obsession to cookies. However, it wasn't the same for me, as I despised this character because I always envied him eating cookies constantly. Cookies were my favorite snack growing up and I would find multiples ways of sneaking a cookie before bedtime. In the show, there were great segments with count dracula, Elmo, and Big bird. And as Cookie Monster scene come up, I feel built up anger, as he eats the cookies or I would call it crumbling and smashing the cookies into pieces. It infuriates me watching the cookie monster waste amazing cookies to the ground. 😡 I've never watched Sesame Street ever again.
